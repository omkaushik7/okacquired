How it started: Brief Story

If there is one thing we have been learning and observing in our previous venture "Legal Salah", that is helping companies owners with all the legal work which is too much struggling work for someone who is not from this background. With the same mindset, we have come up with Ok! Acquired to help people buy and sell their companies effortlessly.

Mission:

On a mission to build a one stop platform where anyone from any corner of the country can buy and sell the companies they find themselves excited about.

Where does the name “Ok! Acquired” come from

Whenever we used to get requests from company owners to help them sell their company, our conversations used to end with this phrase "Ok Acquired" after they received the best deals and from there we decided to take it to another level and present that whole conversation with a better experience.
